# Dialogue Script Template

## Usage
Use this template to write dialogue for cutscenes, boss intros, quest events, or special moments. Each entry can include speaker, context, and optional stage directions.

---

## Example

### [Boss Intro]
SPEAKER: Don Catfishoni
CONTEXT: Player enters the floating market arena
DIALOGUE: "You swim in my waters, you play by my rules!"

### [Quest Completion]
SPEAKER: NPC Fisher
CONTEXT: Player returns with 3 rare carp
DIALOGUE: "You actually caught them? I’m impressed! Here’s your reward."

### [Boss Phase Change]
SPEAKER: Abominable Snowbass
CONTEXT: Player drops Snowbass below 30% health
DIALOGUE: "You think you’ve seen my best? The show’s just getting started!"
